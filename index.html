Test 1

European BEST Engineering Competition (EBEC) is a project conducted by Board of European Students of Technology (BEST), gathering best European students of engineering. With EBEC Project BEST tries to raise the social awareness of all the students in relation to present-day world problems and involve them into resolving those ones. Project brings together students, universities, companies and Non Governmental Organisations.

Project consists of 3 levels and organises a Local BEST Engineering Competition where teams of 4 students compete against each other. The winners of this local event compete against each other in National and Regional BEST Engineering Competitions.
