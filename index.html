BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) is a non-profit, non-political, non-representative students’ organisation with the aim of providing communication, cooperation and exchange possibilities for students of technology since 1989.

European BEST Engineering Competition, pe scurt EBEC, este cea mai mare competiţie de inginerie din Europa, creată de studenţi pentru studenţi pentru a-i aduna cei mai buni studenţi europeni de la inginerie. Echipe formate din 4 studenţi de la profile tehnice, indiferent de anul universitar, sunt provocaţi să rezolve probleme oferite de BEST şi partenerii săi în 3 nivele diferite de competiţie. Echipa care se dovedeşte a fi cea mai eficientă, creativă şi de succes la sfârşitul finalei competiţiei va primi titlul „BEST Engineers of Europe”.

Among 33 countries
Present at 96 universities
More than 4200 active members
Visible to more than 1.700.000 European technical students
BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) is a non-profit and voluntary European student organisation. We strive to provide complementary education for future engineers by communication, cooperation and exchange of students at the most important technical universities and faculties. Complementary education Throughout the year we organise educational, skill developing and leisurencourses for European students. In these courses we give students opportunities to broaden their minds, learn foreign languages, visit diffrent countries and experience new cultures. Educational involvement BEST takes active part in European educational thematic networks, shaping the future of European Higher Education.
